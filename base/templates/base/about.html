About Nephos
We know that making money as a photographer is a challenging thing to do, full of impediments and unpleased clients... The expenses are countless: New camera, new lenses, editing software, time. We know that is hard to monetize this skill, and cause of that we have created the Nelphos platform. We help photographers all around the world to generate a passive income online by selling Lightroom presets, their expression of creativity.

They do this by using our easy-to-use and simple platform. It is designed to be fast so that you can easily navigate and focus on the primary thing: to upload your presets, get sales and make money.

We also want to create a community with the best presets possible, so photographers won’t have to spend hours trying to get the best edit, but choosing a preset from here that will best match it’s needs.
